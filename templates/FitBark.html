FitBark

A Social Dog Walking App

>

Fitbark is an app for connecting local dog owners with one another. Using the local dog community as support, walking is fun & social. FitBark seeks to improve the health of dogs & their human companions. This was created for an Interaction Design Course, focused on creating a community-centered application from ideation through prototype.


Course: SI 582: Introduction to Interaction Design

Date: Sept 2015 - Dec 2015

Tools: Personas, competiive analysis, wireframing, paper prototypes Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, InVision

THE PROBLEM

According to Pet Obesity Prevention, over half the population of North American dogs are overweight. At the same time, most dog owners are also unaware of their dogâ€™s ideal weight. Dogs are completely dependent on humans for fitness, and despite the popularity of fitness trackers, there are none which are dog-centered.

THE SOLUTION

FitBark connects local dog owners by acting as a community hub to encourage walking with social support and achievements. Key aspects include dog-centered profiles for selecting friendly walking companions, people locator, listing users by GPS, a newsfeed to connect and share with friends, sharable achievements on social media, standard messaging, and GPS walking.
